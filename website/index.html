ABOUT

ABOUT with Adele is here to help you stay connected within your community. Enjoy a companion driving service with the peace of mind that comes from being accompanied by a registered nurse – whether you need it or not. I am Police Vetted, CPR trained and hold a passenger endorsed license with NZTA.

I'm Adele, the proud owner of ABOUT – Manawatū's newest companion driving service, established in September 2025. I live near Feilding with my husband on a small farm. Our three adult children have flown the nest. When I'm not driving, I love travelling, gardening, crafting and spending time with family and friends. My loyal companion Maisie is a lovable Cavoodle and often joins me on journeys. If possible (and OK with you) you might even meet her! I am looking forward to meeting new people as we travel along together.

Rates

Rates are based on time and/or distance. When you pre-book, there are no surprises – you will know exactly what you'll be charged at the time of booking.
Some examples of ABOUT’s rates include:

Companion Services

These are charged on time, based on an hourly rate of $50, and are extra to driving rates:

  • Assistance with grocery shopping ($30 first 30 mins, $10 each 15 mins thereafter)
  • Respite care – contact me for a quote
  • Accompanying you on a Doctor’s visit (minimum $20)
  • Scenic drives, beach or café visit – contact me for a quote

Transport Services

To give you an idea of pricing:

  • Feilding or Ashhurst - Palmerston North start from $40 (one way)
  • Within Feilding - minimum charge of $15 (one way)
  • Within Palmerston North - minimum charge of $20 (one way)
  • Palmerston North Hospital/Airport - Levin – approx $120 (one way)

Payment by cash on pick-up or internet banking in advance.
